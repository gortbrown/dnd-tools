Welcome to the D&D Toolbox!

Press f12 to begin!

Type in any of the commands below!

d(num): roll dice with num sides.

dm(num, mod): roll dice with num sides and add mod to it.

setHealth(num): sets health to num.

getHealth(): prints health.

damage(num): take damage by num.

heal(num): heal by num.

character(): open character sheet.

BTW, your card is in Attack On Titan Vol. 34
